Workout Plan

"Push, Pull, Legs" (PPL) is a popular and effective workout routine used in strength training and bodybuilding. It's a way to organize your workouts based on the type of movement patterns and muscle groups involved. The routine is typically structured as follows:

Push

  • Chest-Shoulder-Tricep
  • Bench press
  • Shoulder press
  • Tricep extension
  • Chset fly

Pull

  • Back-Bicep
  • Pull ups
  • Cable rows
  • Bicep curls
  • Deadlift

Legs

  • Quad-Hamstring-Calves
  • Squat
  • Leg extension
  • Hamstring curl
  • Calf raise
